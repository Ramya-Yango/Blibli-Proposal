How Yango Tech accelerates every stage of this curve
Y1 — Hitting 200 orders/day, every store

The WMS and Picker App reduce assembly time by 50% from Week 1, giving stores the throughput capacity to hit 200 OPD in H1 and ramp to 400 OPD by H2. Higher early order quality — fewer cancellations, near-zero missing items — improves retention rates, which drives the H2 volume acceleration needed for the 18-month break-even.

Months 12–18 — Closing in on break-even

Inventory accuracy at 99.5% eliminates write-off losses. Picker KPI tracking reduces headcount waste. Courier handoff optimisation lowers CPO. These operational gains compound across the 8-store network — flattening the loss curve and pulling EBITDA toward zero at the 18-month mark.

Y4+ — Scaling profitably

The same tech stack that ran 1 store scales to 13 without incremental engineering cost. Geo-analytics from Yango Tech's data layer identifies the highest-density catchment areas for stores 6–13, improving GMV per location and driving the Y4 FCF inflection.
